about this route

43 miles - 5,763' Elevation Gain

60% unpaved - 40% paved

By combing two of my favorite routes in my "backyard," this all terrain cycling route plunges you into the Watauga River floodplain and punishes you all the way to the spruce fir ridge of Beech Mountain. Two big climbs, each over 1,500', and three exhilarating descents make for a good day biking in the Northwest corner of North Carolina's High Country.
